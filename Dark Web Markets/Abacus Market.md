## Summary
The Abacus Market links to the new dark web marketplace sections and took over much of the vacuum left by the AlphaBay takedown.

With over 40,000 product listings and valued at around $15 million, it’s a sprawling marketplace and go-to destination for drugs, counterfeit items and [cybercrime](https://cyble.com/blog/data-of-over-59-million-us-residents-available-for-sale-on-a-cybercrime-forum/) tools.

## Summary
Launched in 2023, STYX focuses on financial crime, providing [stolen credit card data](https://cyble.com/blog/massive-ransomware-attack-targets-vmware-esxi-servers/), hacked bank accounts and access to various cryptocurrency laundering tools.

It maintains a very strict level of user verification and integration with an official [Telegram](https://cyble.com/blog/multiple-new-clipper-malware-variants-discovered-in-the-wild/) account to provide real-time updates to users. Various cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin and Monero can be used to make purchases.
